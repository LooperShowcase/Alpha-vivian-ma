if you want to start an editing account, here are some tips:

  • follow many editors and comment on their videos, if you comment on their videos and hype their edits up they will probably do the same, and if they do the same you will get more comments and more people will view it and you will feel more motivated to post
  • mmake mutuals, they hype your videos up and get you more motivated, but make ure to do the same.
  • have good quality, you can search for tutorials on colorings but make sure to give coloring credits if its their coloring
  • dont reuse the same scenes, for example when your editing a character, dont keep using the same clips or scenepack, switch them every 1-5 posts of them.
  • be nice
  • if you remake someones coloring/ edit / original edited audio or something else, always give credits
  • use tags related to your video and popular ones, for example: fyp, viral , the thing you edited. they get you more views
  • use good fonts, you can download cute or famous ones for da.font search it up on google/safari etc...
